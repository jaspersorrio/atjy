WHO AM I?

I am Amanda Tan, an eighteen-year-old girl.

Being able to bless others has always been a joy to me. I am someone that enjoys the presence of people around me and loves interacting with others.

At the same time, I am also a very driven person. I will work towards whatever goals that I set out to accomplish.

However, no matter how far I go in life, humility will always be a value that I hold closely to my heart because I believe that everyone that we meet has something valuable to teach us.

Much more than that, it is also because of my faith. I believe that I am able to stand where I am today is not because of my own abilities, but because of God’s unending grace and love towards me.

I am currently enrolled in Ngee Ann Polytechnic, Diploma in Business & Social Enterprise - a course that allows me to study what I love.

Apart from school, I also do volunteer work to give back to the community in my own little ways, which has also developed my ability to communicate with others effectively.

I am also currently working on a project where I help companies to design their system manuals, allowing me to hone my I.T. & Design skills - something that is out of my discipline.

I aspire to be someone that is able to transform the lives of others because I believe that there is so much more to life than just living for myself!
